About Me

From the court
to the cap table.

I'm a freshman at NYU Stern (GPA 3.84) studying Business, Technology and Entrepreneurship. Before college I attended IMG Academy, one of the world's most selective boarding schools, as a nationally ranked tennis player. I reached #332 in the country and competed in France, Monaco, and Nicaragua.

I started building early. At 15 I founded HyperKickz, a sneaker reselling business I grew from a $100 investment to $310K+ ARR. From there I co-founded Florida's largest sneaker reselling community (200+ paid subscribers, $1M+ in deals), then interned in private equity at American Triple I, supporting underwriting and due diligence on early-stage investments.

Now I'm Partnerships Lead at Phia (AI shopping, backed by Phoebe Gates), where I've closed 850+ brand deals and helped open a new revenue stream with their data analytics platform. I also serve as a Venture Team Analyst at SVS and Investment Analyst at NYU's Blockchain and Fintech Club, evaluating startups for a $200K+ fund.
